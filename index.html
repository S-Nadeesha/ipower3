Company Overview

i Power Technology is an Electrical Engineering Solutions Company currently based in Sri Lanka. We are actively involved in all types of Electrical Engineering Services and our entire team is qualified, well trained and knowledgeable in Industrial as well as Commercial, LV & ELV solution, from design to Installation and Maintenance. We also have specialized experience in the Design, Installation, Maintenance and Service of every Industrial Electrical System. i Power Technology has built a reputation of reliability & high-quality work executed to meet Client's every particular need, using the latest technology.

Our Vision

To become the leading and most reputed Electrical Engineering Service Provider in Sri Lanka, by ensuring that all Electrical Engineering Services are powered by new knowledge and the latest technology.

Our Mission

Fulfillments of the customer exception by providing best services to the customer to achieve highest customer satisfaction through quality service in the best technology.

Ensuring highest employee satisfaction and security of employment, providing opportunities to grow and make a lifetime career with us.

Expanding the business by ensuring social responsivities.
